If you wish to self-host Malak with our Commercial License,
you need to purchase a License Key to do so.

The license can be acquired by sending an email to `support@ayinke.ventures` with the title
or a variant of **Commercial Licensing**.

This is an extremely straight forward process and we try to close this as
soon as possible - ideally in single digit days.
